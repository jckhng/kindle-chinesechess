Kindle ChineseChess
===================

Unofficial Kindle-friendly Xiangqi / Chinese Chess game using a native
GTK2/Cairo interface for jailbroken Kindle devices.

Project lineage
---------------

The Xiangqi rules, piece setup, and built-in AI are adapted from the GPL-3.0
XMuli ChineseChess project:

  https://github.com/XMuli/ChineseChess

Board and piece artwork is copied from the MIT-licensed Augus1217 Chinese-Chess
project:

  https://github.com/Augus1217/Chinese-Chess

The Kindle application shell, Docker build pattern, and KUAL packaging follow
the GnomeGames4Kindle Kindle ports, especially Kindle GlChess and Kindle Iagno.

Pikafish
--------

The app can use Pikafish as an external UCI engine when a compatible executable
is installed as bin/armhf/pikafish. This release may bundle a Kindle ARMv7
hard-float build of the GPLv3 Pikafish engine from:

  https://github.com/official-pikafish/Pikafish

If Pikafish is missing or fails to start, the app falls back to the embedded AI.
When Pikafish is bundled, LICENSES/PIKAFISH-SOURCE.txt records the exact
upstream commit and source/build notes for the binary.
